Project Description

A business-to-business (B2B) web-application that is heavily influenced by Carousell

However, Carousell has many problems such as scammers and sellers cancelling orders after they have been made. Hence, Pepestore aims to solve these issues by acting as a 3rd party ‘middleman’.

This will allow Pepestore to manage and assure that all the products sold are legitimate/legal and that the product will be delivered. This is achieved through a system whereby the seller will send his/her product that is to be sold to the company warehouse and send a request for the listing to be approved. Once an admin approves of the listing, users can chat among themselves to discuss prices and send offers. After the deal is finished, the buyer can put his delivery details and the company will deliver the appropriate products. Users can exchange reviews and feedbacks at the very end of the deal.
