Project Overview

New Hope Community Service(NHCS) is a social service agency that runs shelter for homeless. Currently they have 4 shelters that are located in Margaret Drive, Jalan Kukoh, 1 Spooner and 3 Spooner.

Currently the staff uses paper to record the inspection details and results. This can be be troublesome as they it can be damaged and misplaced easily or find it troublesome finding the past inspection records if required.

Therefore, we have develop a web application called the NHCS Shelter Inspection. It is a responsive web app to record inspection details, schedules the next inspection and generate inspection report.
