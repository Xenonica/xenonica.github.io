Project Overview

HeDeno is a clinic management software where doctors, clinical administrators and patients can better connect with each other, where doctors can better focus on diagnosing patients, clinical administrators can automate tedious paperwork and patients can have a smoother healthcare process. Thus, giving everyone a better healthcare experience on a daily basis.

Today, healthcare workers and patients of different age groups have brought up various issues regarding the current healthcare practices. (e.g. Repetitive paperwork) We aim to fix those issues through the creation of our web app, HeDeno.

On top of that, the healthcare industry has mostly stagnated in its use of traditional practices. (e.g. Onsite queueing) Little attempts have been made to bring the industry online, and even less have been successful at it. We aim to give this push through HeDeno.
