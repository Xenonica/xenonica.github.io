Sports & Wellness Android App

Project Description

Sports & Wellness is a subject that aims to empower students with the fundamental knowledge and skills for a lifelong pursuit of healthy living. This subject allows students to learn practical tips to adopt a healthy lifestyle and be exposed to a broad range of physical activities.

It allows Sports and Wellness lecturers to able to take attendance, calculate IPPT, storing IPPT records, storing student records within a single app. It makes it easier for the lecturers to access. It also provides a platform for students to explore and learn about health, sports and fitness outside of Sports & Wellness lessons.
