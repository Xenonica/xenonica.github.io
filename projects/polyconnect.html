Project Overview

PolyConnect is an Android learning assistant for polytechnic students and lecturers, which aims to integrate AI into education platform to achieve better guidance for students to learn along the learning materials.

Features:

  • AI personalized chatbot
  • Study Planner
  • Grades viewer
  • Flash card system for learning materials
  • AI MCQ generator
  • Calendar and Scheduling System
  • Students insights which allow lecturers to analyze
