Project Overview

Kingdom Come is a gamified learning platform designed to transform academic content into an engaging, interactive kingdom-management experience.

The project reimagines routine educational tasks such as answering questions, recalling facts, and applying concepts as meaningful in-game actions like building structures, gathering resources, and defending a kingdom.

Additionally, this aligns HCI design theme of enhancing learning efficiency and reducing cognitive load through intuitive interaction.


Note: This project is just a concept.
